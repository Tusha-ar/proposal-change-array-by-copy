Repeat, while _k_ < _len_, 1. Let _Pk_ be ! ToString(ùîΩ(_k_)). 1. Let _kPresent_ be ? HasProperty(_originalArray_, _Pk_). 1. If _kPresent_ is *true*, then 1. Let _kValue_ be ?
